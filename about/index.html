About Me

Hi! I'm Brady Folster — an Information Technology & Web Science student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a strong interest in building practical, user-focused technology. I enjoy working at the intersection of engineering, design, and problem-solving, and I'm always looking for ways to make digital experiences simpler and more intuitive, which is taught at a high level at RPI.

Right now, I'm exploring different areas of IT through coursework and hands-on projects, ranging from web development to data handling and UX design. I'm especially interested in roles that let me work across disciplines — collaborating with engineers, understanding user needs, and helping shape how a product evolves.

Long term, I'm working toward a path in product management, where I can combine technical understanding with communication, strategy, and team collaboration. I love thinking about why products work the way they do, how they can be improved, and what features would genuinely help users.

On teams, I'm someone who likes to break down problems, stay organized, and communicate clearly. I enjoy learning new tools, experimenting with small ideas, and supporting the people I work with.

Outside of tech, you can usually find me exploring music, working on personal projects, or keeping up with new apps, tools, and product trends.

If you'd like to connect — or want to talk about opportunities — feel free to reach out!
